Russell Brand tweets picture of wife Katy Perry without any makeup

Here’s one way to annoy your wife.

Comedian Russell Brand posted an unflattering picture today of his wife Katy Perry on his Twitter page that shows the singer without any makeup on.

In the picture, a startled-looking Perry appeared to have been photographed just as she was waking up, London’s The Sun newspaper reported today.

The 26-year-old pop star is never really seen without any makeup.

Perry may have shown her displeasure with Brand, 35, since the picture was removed from his Twitter account.

PHOTOS: KATY PERRY

Brand has 1.7 million Twitter followers, according to his account.

Brand and Perry were married in October in India.

In September, producers of the hit children’s TV show “Sesame Street” canceled an appearance by Perry following feedback from parents.

Perry, who rose to stardom in 2008 with the No. 1 single, “I Kissed A Girl, was set to appear on the premiere of the show’s 41st season.

But after a segment of the show appeared online on Sesame Workshop, which produces the show, decided not to air it.

The segment featured Perry singing a rendition of her 2008 single “Hot N Cold” to the Muppet Elmo. The song, originally about an elusive lover, was rewritten for the clip.

The producers said the show, which has a long history of working with actors, athletes, musicians and artists, has always been written on two levels — for the child and adult.

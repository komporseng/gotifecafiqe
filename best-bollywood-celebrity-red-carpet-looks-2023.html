Bollywood Celebrities Brought Their A-Game To Red Carpets In 2023

2023 is coming to an end and it’s time to express our gratitude for the tireless work of stylists and their teams behind some of the most iconic fashion moments of the year.

With Indian celebrities making waves on a global scale, from the Met Gala to the Oscars, their red carpet style has also stepped up and evolved.

While the usual suspects, like Deepika Padukone, Sonam Kapoor, and Priyanka Chopra, made their way to best dressed lists across the globe, there were many delightful debuts. Anushka Sharma appeared on the Cannes Film Festival red carpet for the first time and wowed everyone with her classic style. Newbies Suhana Khan and Khushi Kapoor, although no strangers to red carpets, put their best sartorial foot forward at the premiere of their movie The Archies. And how can we forget Alia Bhatt’s big Met Gala debut in a Prabal Gurung outfit that paid homage to Karl Lagerfeld?

From Deepika Padukone’s stunning Louis Vuitton velvet gown at the Academy Awards earlier this year to Priyanka Chopra’s Nina Ricci gown at the Love Again premiere, there was no shortage of stunning red carpet moments from our favourite celebrities this year.

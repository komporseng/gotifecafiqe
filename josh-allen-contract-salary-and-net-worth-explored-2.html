Josh Allen contract, salary and net worth explored

Joshua Patrick Allen, popularly known as Josh Allen is a famous American footballer who plays quarterback for the Buffalo Bills of the National Football League. He played college football at Wyoming, where he was a bowl game MVP, and was selected seventh overall by the Bills in the 2018 NFL Draft. He is the son of Lavonne Allen, and Joel Allen and was born to them on  May 21, 1996, in Firebaugh, California, United States. He is 26 years old now. 

In this article, we will dive into his career especially the contract aspect of it to know his contract details with the Bills.

Table of Content

Contract

He was drafted by the Buffalo Bills in the 2018 NFL Draft and has been with the team to date since he has not been traded yet. His first contract with the team expired in 2021.

On August 6, 2021, Allen signed a six-year contract extension worth up to $258 million with $150 million guaranteed.

Salary per year

As per his contract with the team, he will walk home with an average annual salary of $43,005,667. In 2022, Allen will earn a base salary of $4,100,000, a signing bonus of $42,361,405 and a workout bonus of $500,000, while carrying a cap hit of $16,372,281 and a dead cap value of $93,661,405.

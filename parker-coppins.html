Parker Coppins is an American social media star, actor, and television host, best known for hosting the popular television series, ‘Parker Plays.’ Parker Coppins first became popular after gaining fame as a YouTuber.

Early Life and Childhood

Parker Coppins was born on March 12, 1995, in North Carolina, USA. He is 28 years old and is a Pisces. He has sisters named Ashley and Caitlin. Parker moved to Los Angeles, California, in 2015.

He visits his hometown once in a while to see his parents. While his father is a pastor, his mother works for ‘Salazar Insurance Group.’ He is close to his niece Camdyn, who is Ashley’s daughter.

His father’s name is Mark and his mother’s name is Darla Coppins. Parker attended ‘Calvary Christian School.’ Then he attended Harlingen High School.

A local physician named Dr. Leibert was his mentor and inspiration during his school days. Parker often participated in plays with him and that is how he developed a liking for acting and entertainment. He then attended Harlingen High School.

He loves playing video games, so much so that he ended up turning his passion into a profession. Parker is a good friend of some social media star, Shelby. He has featured her in some of his posts on Twitter and Instagram.

Parker Coppins – Professional Life

He created his first and to-be-retired channel ParkerGames on March 30, 2011. And he created his ‘ParkerGames’ YouTube channel on December 1, 2013, and started uploading game-related content.

Because he could turn even ordinary content into a good one, he quickly started getting subscribers. Slowly, his fan base started to grow and Parker began gaining prominence as a social media star.

After appearing as Kyle Duncan in a video titled ‘Born Bad,’ Parker played a minor role in a short film titled ‘Flirting and Dating for Dummies in 2011. In 2013, he appeared in another short film titled ‘Penny Arcade.’

In the same year, he embarked on his social media career as he started posting videos online. And in 2014, he got an opportunity to play Paul Wilson in the crime horror mystery, ‘The Black Tape.’ The film was released in November 2015, marking Parker’s big-screen debut.

2016 and beyond

Moreover, in 2016, he was roped in for the popular TV series ‘Adventure Room,’ in which he played his part under his stage name, ParkerGames.

He was presented with an opportunity to host a show called ‘Parker Plays,’ which was to be aired on Disney XD in 2017. Being a prominent part of a television series increased his popularity, which he used to gather subscribers and followers on his social media pages.

In 2017, he was seen in a short television series titled ‘Sugar Pine 7.’ He appeared in 64 episodes throughout 2017 and continued playing his part till 2018. In the same year, he was cast in a television series titled ‘Lifestyle Classic.’

He had a horse, Jeffery, in a Survival Multi-Player Minecraft world called the Cube SMP. Jeffery was later killed during the CubePurge by another player on the server, Bayani/ xBayani/ Bayanidood.

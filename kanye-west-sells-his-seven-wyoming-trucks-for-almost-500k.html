Kanye West sells his seven Wyoming trucks for almost $500K

After recently listing his Wyoming ranch for $11million, Kanye West has also decided to ditch his ranch vehicles.

The rapper sold his impressive fleet of Wyoming trucks, including Ford Expeditions, Raptors, and Super Duties. Ye even broke a record with his 2019 F-150 Raptor selling for a whopping $86,900 – making it the most expensive Ford Raptor ever sold.

Ye used Musser Bros Auctions to sell his vehicles, with the winning bids for the 7 cars bringing in a total of $434,780, according to an early Monday report from TMZ.

The auction ended on Tuesday with over 137,000 views and over 800 bids. TMZ confirmed that it was the biggest auction ever for Musser Bros.

Kanye accumulated the impressive collection of four-wheel drive trucks and SUVs while living at his ranch in Wyoming.

Ye’s move to sell his vehicles comes after he listed his sprawling 9,000 acre Monster Lake Ranch in Wyoming for sale in October.

Bid deal: The auction ended on Tuesday with over 137,000 views and over 800 bids. TMZ confirmed that it was the biggest auction ever for Musser Bros

The property was originally listed for $14million when the rapper bought it back in September 2019, though it was reported at the time that West, 44, paid under the asking price.

While it is unclear exactly what price the rapper purchased it for, the new asking price is $3million less than what it was when he bought it.

The listing of the ranch and sale of the trucks seems to confirm the recent news about the rapper wanting to be closer to his family which includes estranged wife Kim Kardashian and their four kids North, Saint, Chicago and Psalm.

He changes his homes and look often: The singer at the Vanity Fair Oscar Party in Beverly Hills in 2020

While appearing at the Los Angeles Mission to deliver 1,000 meals to Skid Row ahead of the Thanksgiving holiday last week, Kanye spoke of wanting to be close to his children. 

‘I have to be next to my children as much as possible,’ he said. ‘So, when I’m out the house, I’ve got a house right next to the house. I’m doing everything to be right next to the situation.’ 

According to UsWeekly, one of the alleged breaking points of his marriage to Kim Kardashian was that West wanted his wife to move to Wyoming ‘full time.’ 

Kim has since moved on romantically with SNL vet Pete Davidson.

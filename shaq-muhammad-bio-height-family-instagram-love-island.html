Get To Know Shaq Muhammad! His Height, Family, Love Island

It’s time to brace ourselves as the new set of Love Island contestants is ready to heat up this miserable January. Premiering on 9 pm Monday 16th January 2022 on ITV2 and ITVX, this will be the reality show’s first winter edition since 2020. Shaq Muhammad is one of the singletons entering the villa to hopefully find his future wife there.

In the article below, let’s learn about Shaq’s new stint at Love Island, his age, job, family, dating status, and more.

Shaq Muhammad On Love Island 2023

Compared to the summer edition of Love Island which is filmed in Majorca, Spain,  the winter edition instead takes place in Cape Town, South Africa. Reportedly, the villa of Love Island 2023 is worth more than £5m as it has an Olympic-sized swimming pool, a rugby pitch, a zip line, a 400m running track, and a private lake. Also, Love Island 2023 will be the first series with Maya Jama as a host.

Besides Shaq Muhammad, the other singletons appearing in the ITV show are Tanya Manhenga, Kai Fagan, Lana Jenkins, Ron Hall, Anna-May Robey, Will Young, Tanyel Revan, Olivia Hawkins, and Haris Namani.

Ahead of the show’s premiere, the TV network revealed some information about Shaq, including answers to questions such as why he is on Love Island and what is he looking for in a partner and other things.

Shaq shared that the reason he is entering the Love Island villa is that he hasn’t “had the best luck with previous relationships.” He continued, “So I thought, why not be in the sun and potentially meet the love of my life? Hopefully, I do come out with my future wife. The person I hope to marry one day. Hopefully, she’s in the villa.”

The Love Island 2023 contestant also shared that he still does not know what he’s looking for in a partner. However, he confessed that his family and friends would say that he’s quick to fall in love. Shaq said, ” I’m the type of person that wears my heart on my sleeve. If I find someone I click with, I fall very hard, very fast. Sometimes that can be a bad thing. I’m a very emotional person. It can be a blessing and a curse.”

Shaq also revealed one seemingly lesser-known fact about him. He shared that his favorite film is Dear John and he cries every time he watches that movie.

Shaq Muhammad’s dating status was unclear as of January 2023. The reality TV star rarely shared anything about his dating life on his social media which made it difficult for us to extract any information about his relationships.

However, given that, the reality TV star is on Love Island, we guess he was single at the time of this writing.

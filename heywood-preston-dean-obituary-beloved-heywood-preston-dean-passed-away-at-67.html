Heywood Preston Dean Obituary; Beloved Heywood Preston Dean Passed Away At 67

We wish to express our deepest condolences to the Dean family on the passing of Heywood Preston Dean, who resided in Camden, North Carolina. Born in Elizabeth City, North Carolina, Heywood Preston Dean passed away on December 22, 2023, at the age of 67, leaving behind family and friends who will miss him deeply.

It is encouraged that friends and family members share their sympathies with the family by posting them on this memorial page and sharing them with the family.

His parents, Irvin Elliott Dean and Mary Nell Wright Dean, as well as his brother, William Irvin Dean, passed away before he was born. His brother Keith Dean, who is married to Donna, his niece Heather Ambrose, his nephew Bruce Stallings, and his great-nephews Kai, Brycen, and Brayden are among those who are able to carry on his legacy.

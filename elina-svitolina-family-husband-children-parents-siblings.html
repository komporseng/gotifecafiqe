Elina Svitolina family, husband, children, parents, siblings

Elina Mykhailivna Svitolina is popular as a Ukrainian professional tennis player. She reached career-high rankings of world No. 3 in singles and 108 in doubles. Svitolina has won 17 WTA Tour singles titles and finished runner-up three times.

Table of Content

Elina Svitolina husband

Svitolina is a married woman who got married to ATP tennis player Gaël Monfils. They began their relationship in 2019, announced their engagement on 3 April 2021, and were married on 16 July. Svitolina does not plan on changing her surname with the ITF to Monfils while she is an active player; however, she uses “Elina Monfils” on her social media profiles.

Elina Svitolina children

Svitolina and Monfils announced they were expecting their first child together, a girl on May 15, 2022, and on October 15, they welcomed their daughter, Skaï Monfils, and announced it on their social accounts.

Elina Svitolina parents

Svitolina was born in Odesa to Ukrainian parents Mikhaylo Svitolin, who is a former wrestler, and Olena Svitolina, who is also a former competitive rower. Unusually, she was named after the famous Ukrainian actress Elina Bystritska.

Elina Svitolina siblings

Svitolina has an older brother named Yulian.

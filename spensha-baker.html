Spensha Baker is a young and talented American gospel singer-songwriter. Spensha Baker is most recognized for being one of the finalists of ‘The Voice America’ Season 14.

Early Life

Spensha Baker was born on January 18, 1993, and she is currently 31 years old. Spensha hails from Boerne, Texas, but grew up moving from military base to military base. Likewise, Spensha’s zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Talking about her family, Spensha’s parents are mother Marcy Baker, and father Wayne Baker. Her father Wayne serves in the US Army. Talking about her educational journey, Spensha graduated from Judson High School in San Antonio, Texas. Likewise, she was the captain of her high school softball team.

Spensha Baker – Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Spensha Baker is a famous Gospel singer. She is most recognized as the contestant of the 14th season of the U.S. The Voice. Spensha was a contestant of this American singing competition show in the year 2018 and was also one of the finalists. During the auditions, Spensha sang the song Blackbird by The Beatles.

Before her mainstream fame, Spensha was first seen on-screen in the year 2004. Back then, the singer was a contestant on Star Search. Spensha’s debut album is Outloud! The producer of this album was Tim Miner. She released this album on August 5, 2008.

Likewise, Spensha released Interscope’s 1st gospel record at the young age of 12 years. The singer also sang at the White House for President George W. Bush. Further, Spensha joined fellow singer, Blake Shelton’s team on The Voice. She also gained fame for singing some famous songs like Purpose and Hallelujah.

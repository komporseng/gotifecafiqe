Multiple People Injured In 4-Car Crash In Dracut

The crash happened around 1020 Merrimack Avenue (Route 110) around 9 p.m.

Four cars were involved in the wreck. Police have not released any details about what caused the crash or an update on the victims' conditions. 

Route 110 was closed for several hours Saturday night as officers cleaned, cleared, and investigated the crash site.
